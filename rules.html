Rules:

- Click ‘New Game’ to begin.


- Player 1 goes first. You can roll the dice however many times you wish.


- In the ‘Current’, it gives you the current score as you roll the dice.


- When it hits a certain score, You may want to ‘Hold’ the score, which will then appear as the total score, and You cannot roll anymore in this round. Player 2 begins to roll.


- If the You hit ‘1’ without holding any score, the current score will reset, and the next player begins to roll again.


- Once one of the players hits 20 or more score, that player wins!
